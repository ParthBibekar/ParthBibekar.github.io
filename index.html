Projects

RISoTTo

RRISoTTo (RIbonucleic acid Sequence design from TerTiary structure) is a parameter-free geometric deep learning approach that generates RNA sequences conditioned on both their backbone scaffolds and the surrounding molecular context. A preprint describing RISoTTo has been recently posted on bioRxiv and I presented a poster about it at NeurIPS AI4D3 workshop 2025. More updates with experimental validation will follow soon.

Code Paper

PeSTo-Carbs

PeSTo-Carbs, published in The Journal of Chemical Theory and Computation, is a geometric transformer model extending our earlier work on PeSTo. It is specifically designed to predict binding interfaces between proteins and carbohydrates, including protein–cyclodextrin interactions, with high accuracy.

Welearn-bot

Welearn-bot is a CLI application to interact with welearn in the IISER Kolkata domain. The bot was created and is actively maintained by Satvik, Abhay, Amarjeet and I. It can be used to download all the necessary files from welearn, list your assignments, etc. It has Google Calendar integrated to directly add due assignments to your calendar.

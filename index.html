About

I am a PhD candidate at the Laboratory for Biomolecular Modeling at EPFL. My research interests include applications of machine learning in structural bioinformatics, computational biophysics and integrative modeling. I enjoy playing chess, traveling, reading, and building things.

Projects

RISoTTo

A parameter-free geometric deep learning approach that generates RNA sequences conditioned on both their backbone scaffolds and the surrounding molecular context. Presented at NeurIPS AI4D3 workshop 2025.

Code Paper

PeSTo-Carbs

A geometric transformer model extending PeSTo to predict binding interfaces between proteins and carbohydrates, including protein–cyclodextrin interactions, with high accuracy. Featured as Journal Cover.
